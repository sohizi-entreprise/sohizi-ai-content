# Movie Concepts

**Brief:** A boy is transported into another planet where he has to compete against aliens in solving enigms in order to save the hearth planet.
**Genre:** Sci-Fi
**Duration:** 5 min

## Concept 1: The Cosmic Cipher
**Logline:** A 12-year-old math prodigy is teleported to a neon-lit arena where hyper-logical aliens challenge him to the "Grand Enigma". He must rely on human intuition—a trait the aliens lack—to solve mind-bending spatial puzzles and save Earth from being harvested for cognitive energy.

## Concept 2: Riddle of the Red Sands
**Logline:** Abducted by a desperate, desert-dwelling alien race on a dying planet, adventurous teen Elias must navigate the ancient "Labyrinth of the Elements." He competes against alien champions in puzzles that require Earthly empathy and an understanding of nature, proving humanity's worth to spare his world.

## Concept 3: Neon Labyrinth
**Logline:** Sucked into a virtual-reality-like alien dimension, a street-smart boy named Jax is forced into the "Starlight Games"—a galactic tournament of high-speed, holographic enigmas. Realizing the game is rigged to destroy Earth, Jax must team up with a rogue alien competitor to hack the system from within.
